\section{Preliminaries}
As aforementioned, to estimate a utility direction we propose multiple methods. One of which requires the mean
of the activations on varied helpfulness scores on HelpSteer \citet{wang-etal-2024-helpsteer}. To do this we first
take a sample of HelpSteer to estimate the mean, in this section we aim to find a lower bound for the sample size 
to get a good estimate of the mean.
